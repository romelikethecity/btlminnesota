BTL Event Registration System

A reusable event registration template that drives registrations, reduces no-shows, and scales across any BTL region. This walkthrough covers every feature.

Feature walkthrough

Click through each feature in order. The full registration flow takes about 60 seconds.

Main Landing Page

Value-driven hero with verifiable BTL stats ($200K+ avg annual revenue, 87% satisfaction, 30-min sessions). Credibility strip, ROI section, agenda, speakers, and FAQ — all customized per event and region.

URL Pre-fill

Every registration link can include the physician's name, email, and practice. One click, the form is already filled — just hit submit. We generate these from your CRM export.

4

Submit → Confirmation

Submit any form (mock — no real backend yet). You'll see the confirmation page with personalized greeting, event details, and next steps.

Go to Form
5

Calendar Integration

After registration, the confirmation page offers Google Calendar and .ics download. Timezone-aware, includes 1-day and 2-hour reminders. Reduces no-shows.

Available on the confirmation page after submitting a form.

6

Referral Sharing

Each registrant gets a unique referral URL. Copy button, email, SMS, and native share (mobile). Referred registrations are tracked — every attendee recruits more attendees.

Available on the confirmation page after submitting a form.

7

Social Proof + Scarcity

"47 providers already registered" (main) / "14 chiropractors already registered" (specialty). Capacity meter shows spots remaining. Counts update from config for demo, from API in production.

Visible on the main page and specialty pages when thresholds are met.

8

Exit Intent + Sticky CTA

Desktop: overlay triggers when cursor moves toward the browser close area, or after 45 seconds of inactivity. Mobile: sticky CTA bar appears at 60% scroll depth. Only fires once per session, only for unregistered visitors.

Wait 45 seconds on any page, or move your cursor toward the top of the browser.

9

Post-Event Mode

After the event: registration form hides, replaced by a lead magnet download CTA. One config toggle flips every page. Add ?post=true to any URL to preview it.

Preview Post-Event
10

Registrant Data Pipeline

Every registration syncs to our master database. We match registrants to existing contacts, flag new prospects, and track who actually attends. Post-event, you get a full report: who registered, who showed, who's a new lead, and who's ready for follow-up.

Automated sync — no manual CSV exports or CRM imports.
